ЁЯУЛ Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Muslim Prayer ("we," "our," or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Muslim Prayer is designed to help Muslims perform their religious obligations by providing accurate prayer times, Quran, Qibla direction, and Islamic calendar features. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe user experience.

ЁЯФТ Privacy First

Muslim Prayer is a 100% offline app. All your data stays on your device. We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal information with third parties.

ЁЯУК Information We Collect

1. Location Data

  • Purpose: To calculate accurate prayer times based on your geographical location
  • Type: GPS coordinates or manually selected city/district
  • Storage: Location data is stored locally on your device only
  • Sharing: We do NOT share your location data with any third parties

2. Device Information

  • Type: Device model, Android version, app version
  • Purpose: To ensure app compatibility and improve performance
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device

3. User Preferences

  • Type: Selected city/district, notification settings, prayer calculation methods
  • Purpose: To provide personalized prayer time notifications
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device

ЁЯОп How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  1. Calculate Prayer Times: Using your location to provide accurate prayer times for your area
  2. Send Notifications: To remind you of prayer times with Azan notifications
  3. Qibla Direction: To show accurate Qibla (Kaaba) direction from your location
  4. Improve App Performance: To fix bugs and enhance user experience

ЁЯТ╛ Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All your data is stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Storage: We do NOT store your data on any external servers
  • Offline Functionality: The app works completely offline, with 100 years of prayer time data pre-loaded
  • No Data Collection: We do NOT collect, transmit, or sell your personal data to third parties
тЪая╕П No Third-Party Services

Muslim Prayer does NOT use any third-party services that collect user data:

  • тЭМ No advertising networks
  • тЭМ No analytics services (like Google Analytics)
  • тЭМ No social media integrations
  • тЭМ No cloud services

ЁЯФС Permissions Required

The app requires the following Android permissions:

1. Location Permission (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION)

  • Purpose: To determine your location for accurate prayer times and Qibla direction
  • Optional: You can deny this permission and manually select your city

2. Notification Permission (POST_NOTIFICATIONS)

  • Purpose: To send prayer time reminders and Azan notifications
  • Optional: You can disable notifications in app settings

3. Vibration Permission (VIBRATE)

  • Purpose: To vibrate during Azan notifications
  • Optional: Can be disabled in settings

ЁЯС╢ Children's Privacy

Muslim Prayer is suitable for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the app stores all data locally and does not transmit any data, children's privacy is fully protected.

тП▒я╕П Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data remains on your device until you uninstall the app
  • You can clear app data anytime through Android settings
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all local data

тЬЕ Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  1. Access: View all data stored in the app settings
  2. Modify: Change location, notification preferences anytime
  3. Delete: Uninstall the app to remove all data
  4. Opt-out: Disable location and notification permissions in Android settings

ЁЯФД Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page. The "Last Updated" date at the top will be revised. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
